Why Educators use AInterview

Asynchronous Review

Stop scheduling back-to-back interviews during class. Review transcripts and recordings at your own pace.

Voice Verification

Listen to student responses directly in the chat to verify authenticity and assess tone.

Consistent Experience

Prompta never fatigues. Every student gets the same attentive interviewer, even after 30 interviews.

Focus on Feedback

Save hours of class time to focus purely on grading and providing high-quality feedback.

Downloadable Records

Export full transcripts as text files or download entire class sets as a ZIP archive.

Executive Function

Set strict time limits (3-15 mins) to help students hone concise thinking and time management.

Ontario Curriculum

Optimized for "Learning for Success" assessment standards in Speaking & Listening.

How It Works

From setup to grading in five simple steps.

1

Create Account

Sign up for a free Teacher Account to access your secure dashboard.

2

Upload Context

Upload a PDF or paste your curriculum text directly. (Refer to exemplars below).

3

Configure

Set the interview duration (e.g., 10 mins) and enable optional voice recording.

4

Assess

Generate a unique code and share the student link to begin the interview.

5

Review

In your teacher account, review transcripts, listen to audio (premium feature).

Privacy & Terms of Use

1. Who We Are

This tool was developed by Jonathan Tepper for use within Greenwood College School to explore AI-driven academic assessment.

2. Open Source & Attribution

This software is provided as open-source educational software under the MIT License. Educators are free to use and modify this code, provided that original attribution to Greenwood College School and Jonathan Tepper remains intact.

3. Data Privacy (Student Data)

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Audio: Your voice is processed via Google Cloud. Recordings are stored securely for teacher verification and are automatically permanently deleted after 9 months (270 days).
  • Transcripts: Text logs are stored securely for your teacher to review.
  • AI Processing: Data sent to the AI is for the sole purpose of this assessment and is not used to train public models.

4. Consent

By using this tool, you consent to the recording of your voice for the specific purpose of this oral assessment.
